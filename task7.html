Sai Pallavi Senthamarai (born May 9, 1992) is an Indian actress and dancer who primarily works in Telugu, Tamil, and Malayalam films.

Krithi Shetty (born 21 September 2003)[1] is an Indian actress who primarily works in Telugu and Tamil films. Shetty is an recipient of a Filmfare Award South and a SIIMA Award.

Anupama Parameswaran (born 18 February 1996[1]) is an Indian actress who primarily works in Telugu, Malayalam and Tamil films.[2]

Tamanna Bhatia[a] (born 21 December 1989), known professionally as Tamannaah Bhatia, is an Indian actress who predominantly works in Telugu, Tamil and Hindi films.
